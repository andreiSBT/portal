About Fiascha

Fiascha is a micronation founded on December 21, 2022, dedicated to education, culture, and innovation.

Our nation brings together citizens from around the world who share common values of learning, collaboration, and creativity. We emphasize multilingual education (Romanian, English, French, German), strategic thinking (Chess), and cultural exchange.

As a micronation, we focus on building a strong community identity through our symbols, history, and shared goals for the future.
