The Flag of Fiascha

The national flag is a vertical tricolor representing our values and the natural beauty of our nation.

Design Specifications:
  • Format: Vertical tricolor (three equal vertical stripes)
  • Proportion: 2:3 (width to length)
  • Colors: Blue, Orange-Yellow, Green (from left to right)
Symbolism:
  • Blue (Left) - Represents knowledge, education, and the endless pursuit of learning
  • Orange-Yellow (Center) - Symbolizes energy, creativity, and the bright future ahead
  • Green (Right) - Represents growth, harmony, and our commitment to sustainability

Symbol Usage Guidelines

National symbols are important representations of Fiascha and should be used respectfully:

Permitted Uses:
  • Official government communications
  • Educational materials about Fiascha
  • Citizen profile displays
  • Community events and celebrations
  • News articles and announcements
Please Avoid:
  • Distorting or modifying official symbols
  • Using symbols for commercial purposes without permission
  • Disrespectful or inappropriate usage
  • Misrepresenting Fiascha's identity
Questions? Contact the administration if you're unsure about symbol usage.
